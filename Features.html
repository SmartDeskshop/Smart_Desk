Smart Shop Manager - Features

All-in-one dashboard for your shop, smart & simple.

Core Features

Inventory Management
Track stock in real-time, update quantity, price, and delete items anytime.
Customer Tracking
Maintain detailed customer records including purchase history and contact info.
Sales Analytics
Visual graphs and reports to analyze revenue, expenses, and growth trends.
Billing System
Generate professional invoices, print or download as PDF, with customer info.
Smart Notifications
Get alerts for low stock, due payments, new customers, and more in real-time.
Secure Login
Firebase Authentication with email login and protected admin access.
Cloud Sync (Firestore)
Auto-sync data in real-time with Google Firebase — secure, fast, and reliable.
Export & Backup
Download your data in spreadsheet format for records, backup, or reports.
Mobile-Friendly
Fully responsive layout works perfectly on mobile phones and tablets.
