Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2025

SmartDesk is committed to customer satisfaction. We've established this transparent cancellation and refund policy to ensure fair and consistent treatment for all our customers while maintaining the highest standards of service.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations will be considered only if the request is made within 3-5 days of placing the order.
  • Cancellation may not be possible if the order has already been processed by our vendors and the fulfillment process has begun.
  • For perishable items, cancellations are generally not accepted once the order is placed, though quality issues will be addressed appropriately.

Damaged or Defective Items

Please report any damaged or defective items to our Customer Experience team within 3-5 days of delivery. We'll initiate an investigation with the supplier and resolve the matter promptly.

Product Not as Described

If the product received doesn't match the website description or fails to meet quality expectations, please notify us within 3-5 days of receipt. We'll work with you to find a satisfactory resolution.

Manufacturer Warranty

Products covered by manufacturer warranties should be addressed directly with the manufacturer for service or replacement requests.

Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 3-5 business daysStandard Processing to the original payment method. The timing of the refund appearing in your account depends on your financial institution.

Note: This policy is established at the sole discretion of SmartDesk Management. Payment processors including Razorpay are not responsible for merchant policies or any liabilities arising from them.
