Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our product and services

What is Smart Shop Manager?
Smart Shop Manager is a comprehensive retail management solution designed to streamline your shop operations. Our platform offers inventory management, point of sale, customer relationship tools, and advanced analytics - all in one intuitive dashboard.
How do I add new products to my inventory?

To add new products:

  1. Navigate to the "Inventory" section in your dashboard
  2. Click "Add New Product"
  3. Fill in product details (name, SKU, price, quantity, etc.)
  4. Upload product images (optional)
  5. Click "Save"

Your product will be immediately available for sales and inventory tracking.

Can I generate bills for customers?

Yes, our billing system allows you to:

  • Create professional invoices with your shop branding
  • Apply discounts and taxes automatically
  • Save customer information for future purchases
  • Print receipts or email them directly to customers
  • Track payment status (paid, pending, partial)
Where is my data stored and how secure is it?

Your data security is our top priority:

  • All data is stored in secure, encrypted Firebase Firestore databases
  • We use enterprise-grade AWS servers with 99.9% uptime
  • Regular automated backups ensure no data loss
  • Role-based access control lets you manage staff permissions
  • GDPR compliant data handling practices
How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link (valid for 1 hour)
  4. Create a new strong password

If you don't receive the email, please check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Can I access the dashboard offline?

Smart Shop Manager offers limited offline functionality:

  • Process sales and generate bills (will sync when back online)
  • View product inventory (last synced data)
  • Access customer information

For full functionality including real-time stock updates and reporting, an internet connection is required.

How often is my data backed up?

We implement a robust backup strategy:

  • Real-time replication: All changes are instantly copied to multiple servers
  • Daily snapshots: Complete system backups every 24 hours
  • 30-day retention: Ability to restore data from any point in the last month
  • Export options: Download your data anytime in CSV or Excel format

You can also manually trigger backups from your account settings.
